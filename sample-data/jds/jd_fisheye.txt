FishEye Software has an immediate opening for a skilled C++ Software Engineer to support the Cooperative Engagement Capability (CEC) Program. In this role, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to support system integration, testing, and validation, helping deliver a unified and accurate tactical picture.

This position requires an Active Secret Clearance and is 100% on-site in Maynard, MA.

• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define software requirements, interfaces, and integration plans.
